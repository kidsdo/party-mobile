*Why Do My Balloons Burst?

Bursting may be caused by many factors such as static electricity brought about by changing weather conditions, or may occur under extreme heat, rapid weather changes, or caught by something sharp. While we use the highest quality balloons and take every precaution necessary with your balloons to ensure they last through your event, once the balloons leave our door or we have left the venue, they are out of our control. We cannot guarantee that every balloon will remain intact for the duration of the event.
